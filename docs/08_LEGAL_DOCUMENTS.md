# 3. Community Guidelines

## Backroom Community Guidelines

Backroom is built on trust, empathy, and respect. These guidelines help maintain a safe and supportive community.

### Our Values

**Empathy** - We listen without judgment
**Respect** - We treat everyone with dignity
**Safety** - We protect each other
**Anonymity** - We preserve each other's privacy

### Expected Behavior

#### As a Sharer:
- Be honest in your preview (topic, tone, intent)
- Respect the listener's time and boundaries
- Avoid sharing identifying information (names, locations)
- Accept that listeners cannot solve all problems
- Thank listeners who help you

#### As a Listener:
- Listen actively and empathetically
- Respect the sharer's experience
- Don't offer unsolicited advice unless asked
- Know your limits and take breaks
- Report concerning behavior

### Prohibited Behavior

#### Zero Tolerance (Immediate Ban):
- Sexual harassment or explicit content without consent
- Threats of violence
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Attempting to identify or "dox" another user
- Recording calls
- Sharing another user's information

#### Serious Violations (Warning, then Ban):
- Harassment or bullying
- Repeatedly lying in previews
- Ignoring listener boundaries
- Abusive language
- Spam or promotional content

#### Minor Violations (Warning):
- Accidentally sharing identifying information
- Ending calls abruptly without reason
- Not responding to previews (if available)

### Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation:
1. Use the in-app report feature
2. Select the appropriate category
3. Provide any relevant details
4. We will investigate within 24 hours

All reports are confidential.

### Enforcement

We take a graduated approach to enforcement:

1. **First minor violation** - Warning
2. **Second minor violation** - Temporary restriction
3. **Third minor violation** - Account suspension
4. **Serious violation** - Immediate suspension pending review
5. **Zero tolerance violation** - Permanent ban

We reserve the right to skip steps for serious violations.

### Appeals

If you believe you were unfairly penalized:
1. Email appeals@backroom.app
2. Include your anonymous user ID
3. Explain your situation
4. We will review within 7 days

### Mental Health Note

Backroom is a peer support platform, not a mental health service. If you are experiencing:
- Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
- A mental health crisis
- An emergency situation

Please contact professional help:
- Befrienders Kenya: +254-722-178-177
- Emergency: 999 or 112

### Thank You

Thank you for being part of the Backroom community. Together, we can create a space where everyone feels heard.
